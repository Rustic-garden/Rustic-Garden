Our Coffee Story

Three generations of passion on Mount Kenya

Born from Volcanic Soil

Our story begins with a small family plot where coffee bushes thrived under indigenous shade trees. What started as subsistence farming has grown into a mission: to produce exceptional coffee that respects nature, empowers people, and connects every customer to the heart of Kavutiri, Embu.

1,800m Elevation
1952 Family Farm Founded
100+ Families Supported
3 Generations

Perfect Terroir

Mount Kenya's volcanic soils, cool misty mornings, and steady sunshine create ideal growing conditions. Our microclimate produces beans with exceptional complexity and sweetness.

Community Impact

Every purchase supports local families through fair wages, youth training programs, and community development projects. Coffee farming that builds stronger communities.

Sustainability First

Organic cultivation, shade-growing, and water stewardship protect our environment for future generations while producing better coffee today.

Farm to Cup Process

Where tradition meets innovation

Our Cultivation Method

🌱

Organic Growing

Natural compost, green manure, and beneficial microorganisms enrich our soil without synthetic chemicals. Healthier plants produce better coffee.

🌳

Shade Cultivation

Coffee thrives under indigenous tree canopies, protecting delicate beans while supporting biodiversity. Birds, insects, and wildlife flourish in our farm.

💧

Water Stewardship

Rainwater harvesting and targeted drip irrigation ensure responsible water use. Every drop counts in sustainable farming.

Harvest & Processing

1️⃣

Hand-Picking

Only the ripest red cherries are selected during harvest season.

2️⃣

Honey Processing

Natural mucilage retained during processing enhances sweetness and body.

3️⃣

Sun Drying

Raised beds allow even airflow, developing complex flavors naturally.

4️⃣

Quality Control

Each batch is cupped and graded to ensure consistent excellence.
