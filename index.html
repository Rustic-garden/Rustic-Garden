Two-Week Family Box — Seed to Plate

Balanced selections designed to keep food fresh for up to 14 days when stored responsibly. Sourced from our coffee terraces, fish ponds, macadamia groves and vegetable beds.

Fresh Coffee (roast-to-order) • 500g — keeps flavour for 10–14 days if sealed
Tilapia (whole) • 2–3 fish — best within 5–7 days chilled or frozen for longer
Macadamias (shelled) • 300g — shelf-stable 10–14 days in cool place
Root Vegetables (sweet potatoes, nduma) • 3–5kg — store in dry, cool place — lasts 10–14+ days
Fruits (bananas, grafted pears, tree tomatoes) • mixed — stagger eating order: apples & pears later, bananas earlier
Herbs & greens (for daily use) • rotate and keep wrapped in damp cloth — refresh at day 7

We craft each box for freshness and sustainability — notify us of dietary needs. Deliveries across Embu County.

Packaging includes
  • Insulated liner + ice packs for fish & perishable items
  • Biodegradable boxes and labels
  • Cooking suggestions & storage guide inside
