About Rustic Garden

Rustic Garden is a dynamic agricultural and training hub overlooking Mount Kenya in Kavutiri, Embu. We specialize in premium coffee, macadamia, aquaculture, and sustainable farming solutions. Our site features a swimming pool, a section for landscapers, and facilities for dam and water pan construction. We are deeply committed to youth empowerment through training, sponsorship, and business development programs.
