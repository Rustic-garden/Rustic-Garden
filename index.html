About Rustic Garden

Rustic Garden is a dynamic agricultural and training hub overlooking Mount Kenya in Kavutiri, Embu. We specialize in premium coffee, macadamia, and aquaculture, all produced with a strong commitment to sustainable farming. Our site features a swimming pool, a section for landscapers, and facilities for dam and water pan construction. We are deeply committed to youth empowerment through training, sponsorship, and business development programs. We also provide a sanctuary for wellness and connection with nature, offering unique experiences like sylvotherapy and chlorine-free swimming.

Wellness & Nature Experiences

Embrace Sylvotherapy

Unlock the power of trees and boost your well-being. Our garden provides a sanctuary to reduce stress, improve focus, and spark creativity through the ancient practice of sylvotherapy.

Chlorine-Free Swimming

Take a dip in serenity. Our eco-friendly, chlorine-free pool is perfect for a gentle workout or a relaxing swim, offering a low-impact way to stay active and connected with nature.

Sustainable Irrigation

Ditch traditional methods and upgrade to our water-wise drip irrigation system. It delivers water directly to plant roots, minimizing evaporation and runoff while boosting plant growth and health.
