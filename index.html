Children of Kembu

We are the descendants of Mwenendega, who first saw the beautiful Nveta bathing by the sacred waters of Mbui Njeru. From their union came Kembu and Werimba, whose children became the Embu people. Our land stretches from the slopes of Mt. Kenya, where Ngai dwells, down to the fertile valleys at 4,000 feet.

For generations, our athamaki (elders) have guided our communities through the wisdom of ciama councils. At Rustic Garden, we honor this heritage by creating spaces where tradition meets innovation, where the sacred waters still flow, and where every visitor becomes part of our extended family.
