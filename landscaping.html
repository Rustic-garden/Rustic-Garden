How the Customer Journey Works

A simple, step-by-step process from our first call to a flourishing new landscape.

1

Discovery

A free 30-min call or site visit to take quick measurements and discuss your priorities.

2

Model & Proposal

We provide a 3D/AR preview of your space, a phased proposal, and a social impact plan.

3

Implementation

We use circular materials, low-impact machinery, and on-site youth apprentices for installation.

4

Wellness Activation

We guide you in activating new meditation and biodiversity areas for your household or staff.

5

Maintenance & Reports

Optional subscription maintenance and quarterly Atmosphere Health Reports to track your progress.

6

Showcase & Community

Share your impact story and become a community demo site to inspire others.

Impact & Social Value

  • Water saved (litres/year)

Social Impact

  • Youth apprentices employed & certified
