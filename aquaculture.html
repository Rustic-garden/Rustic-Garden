Product Portfolio

Five turnkey offerings to fit your scale and goals. Combine modules to build a closed‑loop, profitable system.

AquaPro™ Tank System

A starter kit for schools & smallholders. It offers simple ops, fast setup, and is upgrade‑ready to our USB‑MDR loop.

NutriFlow™ Water Treatment

Expanded clay pellets for high-surface area biofiltration and denitrification. A drop‑in solution for existing RAS.

AquaGen™ Feed Management

Sensor-based feed control to optimize feed conversion and reduce waste, improving overall system efficiency.

EcoHarvest™ Filtration

An integrated solid waste filter that captures and processes sludge into high-value fertilizer.

System Integrator Service

Expert-led design and installation of turnkey aquaculture systems for industrial-scale operations.
