Case Study: 100 MT/year Tilapia RAS with USB‑MDR

Stocking 70 g → harvest 845 g in 24 weeks across 12 tanks (20.5 m³ each). Two phases with tank splitting at week 12; sawtooth feed pattern; max feed 349 kg/day; average 333 kg/day.

Farm Profile

  • Species: Oreochromis niloticus
  • Annual output: 100 MT (~118,946 fish)
  • Survival: ~99.5%
  • FCR: ~1.34

Culture Plan

  • 12 tanks in blocks of 3
  • Stock every 3 weeks (~6,862 fish)
  • Phase 1: 70 → 370 g (12 weeks)
  • Phase 2: 370 → 845 g (12 weeks)

Operations

  • Max density design ≈ 140 kg/m³
  • Water quality stabilized by USB‑MDR
  • Lower discharge of N, P, COD
