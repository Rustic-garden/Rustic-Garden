From volcanic soils and cool mountain mists, our macadamias grow alongside shade-loving coffee, then travel a gentle path—hand-picking, careful cracking, and slow sun-drying—so the first snap you hear is matched by a clean, buttery finish you remember.

Orchards & Varieties

In the rich volcanic belts of Mount Kenya, macadamia integrifolia and tetraphylla settle their roots among arabica rows, the shade softening wind stress, the leaf litter feeding earth life, the bees moving between white coffee bloom and macadamia racemes to stitch a small ecology into every kernel.

Harvest, Shelling, and the Gentle Path

Ripeness is read in the drop and the husk, hands gather what the tree releases, then shells meet calibrated pressure—enough to open, never enough to bruise—so more kernels arrive whole and more flavour reaches the table.

Hand-Picking at Peak

Selective collection ensures uniform moisture and clean sweetness.

Gentle Mechanical Cracking

Pressure-matched cracking preserves integrity and reduces waste.

Careful Sorting & Grading

Colour, size, and sound guide grading before drying begins.

Drying & Storage

Sun does what it does best, carrying moisture down to the narrow window where texture sharpens and oils stay luminous; after that, climate-controlled calm keeps every lot steady until it travels.

Sun-Finished Drying

Natural sweetness preserved, crisp break achieved without harsh heat.

Climate-Steady Stores

Kernel freshness held with low-oxygen barriers and clean air flow.
