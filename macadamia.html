The Journey of a Nut

Harvest

Nuts are harvested from the orchard floor, not from the tree, ensuring they are fully mature and ripe.

Drying

We use a specific drying curve to reduce moisture content and extend shelf life while maintaining texture and flavor.

Sorting & Grading

Nuts are sorted by size, kernel percentage, and color to meet our high export-quality standards.
