Family Nutrition Pack

Purpose & Story

Designed for families facing chronic illness, our Nutrition Pack provides wholesome foods to support immunity, recovery, and day-to-day vitality. Inspired by stories from our community, each pack is a gesture of care and resilience.

Contents

  • Charlie's Coffee (fresh-roasted, whole bean or ground)
  • Premium macadamia nuts
  • Arrow roots (nduma)
  • Natural dried bananas
  • Seasonal vegetables (locally sourced)

Nutritional Design

Each item is chosen for its contribution to immune support, energy, and digestive health—balancing proteins, healthy fats, complex carbs, vitamins, and minerals.

Delivery Options

  • Home delivery (weekly, biweekly, or monthly)
  • Subscription plans for ongoing support
