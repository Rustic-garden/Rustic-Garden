Events at Rustic Garden

Celebrate culture, wellness, and nature. Join us for unforgettable experiences in the heart of Embu.

Application of Reality and Imagination

Reality plane: Eating to live, coffee to fuel, fish to nourish — the Festival grounds you in necessity.

Imagination plane: Choirs rising in the garden, weddings under open skies, laughter around the pool — these gestures transform necessity into abundance, pride, and the dream of flourishing.

Wellness Retreats

Join guided sylvotherapy walks, meditation, and renewal...

Breathe deep among trees, let silence frame your inner world. The Festival nudges you to imagine your life as whole, not fragmented.

Coffee Tastings

Experience Charlie’s Coffee with guided tastings...

Picture Vivaldi’s *Four Seasons* in the garden, fresh tilapia sizzling in the pan, moka pots bubbling with Embu coffee. Weddings, choirs, birthdays — each becomes a stage for abundance.

Aquaculture Showcase

Explore our tilapia and catfish ponds...

Fish to fuel, but also fish to feast. By the pool, abundance reframed as joy, necessity elevated into cultural imagination.

Community Festivals

Celebrate Embu heritage through food and dance...

School choirs, church groups, birthdays, weddings — ordinary gatherings reframed as constitutional pride, binding reality to imagination.
