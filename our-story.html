Our Story

Rooted in Embu, where mountain rains meet fertile soil and community traditions, Rustic Garden blends heritage with innovation. Our coffee, aquaculture, and landscapes are shaped by the stories of the past — and the sustainable future we’re writing together.

Wellness & Sylvotherapy

Discover sylvotherapy — the healing power of trees. Reduce stress, boost immunity, and spark creativity by reconnecting with nature at Rustic Garden.

Aquaculture & Food Security

We raise tilapia and catfish using eco-friendly ponds, boosting food security, job creation, and environmental sustainability.

Charlie’s Coffee

From the fertile slopes of Kavutiri, our honey-processed Arabica beans bring a sweet, rich cup of Kenyan excellence, hand-picked and carefully roasted.

Water Innovation

Our drip irrigation and water harvesting systems conserve resources, boost yields, and nurture the land for generations to come.
